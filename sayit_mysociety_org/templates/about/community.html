How to get involved with SayIt

SayIt was built it for other people to use. If you'd like to get involved, here's where to start. We’re looking for volunteers, whether you have coding skills or transcripts you’d like to upload.

If you’re technically minded, please read our introduction for developers, including information on the data standard we use for transcripts and how you can help.

If you’re not technically minded, please look at our guide for getting transcripts online, and of course please play with our site and let us know your thoughts.

Contact

  • Say hello on Twitter or Facebook.
  • Join the Poplus mailing list, a discussion area for the various components contained in Poplus.
  • Email us at sayit@mysociety.org.

Source code

The source code to SayIt is avaliable in our GitHub repo, licensed under the Affero GPL.

The data standard we are using is a subset of Akoma Ntoso – a standard developed specifically for use in parliaments and assemblies.
